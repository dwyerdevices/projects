## More Clips, or How to Finish off a Spool

What to do with the last meter or two of filament on a spool? I print more filament clips, as I'm 
_constantly_ losing these things. This batch was printed to fit Inland/eSun spools, and just about
perfectly finished off a spool of light brown PLA.
